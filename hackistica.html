F A Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Here we address some of the most frequently asked queries of the participants regarding the hackathon. Please go through this section to get an epitomized understanding of everything you need to know.

01. What is the workflow/timeline of the event?

The registrations will begin on 14th May and close on 18th May EOD. The coding phase of the hack will take place from May 20, 2022 to May 22, 2022. The results are expected to be declared latest by 25th May 2022.

02. What is the team size? Is solo participation allowed?

The minimum team size must be 2 whereas the maximum team size is limited to 3.

03. When will the Main Tracks be released?

The main tracks will be released at the start of the coding phase, i.e, on 20th May.

04. Who can participate?

We invite participation from everyone who has a knack for working on practical problems related to Development.

05. What are the perks of participating in the event?

The winners shall stand to win prizes in excess of INR 50,000 .

06. Where can we reach for further questions?

Participants are required to join the discord channel for posting any queries and issues with regards to this hackathon. Any minor updates regarding the event will also be posted therein.

07. What if I am a novice or don’t have any ideas?

That’s normal. Most people don’t have an idea before they get to the event. Even if you are a novice, you may work in a team, which may prove to be your ultimate chance to learn. Who knows, relishing your goals, you may hit the jackpot! Protip: Invite your friends to come to the hackathon too! That way you’re guaranteed to have at least one familiar face.

08. What will be the judging criteria?

The judging criteria will be released along with the problem statement, i.e, on 20th May.

09. Can I participate in more than one problem tracks?

Though not mandated as such, it is highly encouraged for you to make your submissions under the Main Tracks. However, you can also pursue the Sponsor Tracks concurrently to maximize your rewards. In case you wish to pursue the Sponsor Tracks concurrently, you are technically free to do so. However, preference will be given to those working under Main Tracks in deciding the grand winner.

10. Can I join more than one team?

No. You can join only one team. If found as a part of more than one team, your participation will be rendered void.

The hackathon is aimed to attract students from colleges all over India to ideate and implement their development skills in amalgamating AI/ML with web development to solve the various problem statements.
